XProc 3.0 Plotter

A simple preview of an XProc 3.0 pipeline step declaration or library.

Load your XProc 3.0 below: the application does its best to process and display it. All processing is in your browser.

Feedback on this demonstration is welcome on the public Github Issues board.

Users of XProc 3.0 may also be interested in the project OSCAL XProc 3.0.

About XProc 3.0

XProc 3.0 is the latest generation of W3C XProc XML pipelining technology.

This viewer application is maintained on Github, with documentation.
